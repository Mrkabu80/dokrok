Research

From hypothesis to responsible application.

At DOK ROK, research is not a decorative promise. It is a structured process involving a question, evidence, biological plausibility, quality and transparent communication.

Research perspective

A human being is more than a measurement.

Biological mechanisms, clinical context, nutrition, lifestyle, comorbidities and individual circumstances need to be considered together.

The NutraMedicine approach explores interfaces between conventional medicine, biochemistry, genetics, micronutrients and selected natural substances. The decisive question is not whether something is “natural” or “technical”, but whether it is traceable, safe, quality-assured and relevant to the question at hand.

Research pathway

Four levels for robust concepts.

Problem & need

Define medical relevance, target population and the existing care gap.

Mechanism & evidence

Systematically assess biological plausibility, literature, risks and available data.

Concept & quality

Connect formulation, dosage, manufacturing, analytics and documentation.

Evaluation & learning

Measure results, state limitations and continuously develop the insight.

Fields of interest

Research at the interfaces.

Oral health, neurology, micronutrients, fungal biology, rare diseases, medical ethics and new dosage concepts.

Genetics & cell biology

Understand mechanisms, distinguish similarities and critically evaluate transferability.

Clinical relevance

Relate research to patient benefit, safety and real care situations.

Quality & transparency

Make evidential limitations visible and formulate claims responsibly.
